What is RPA?

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is a transformative technology that uses software robots or "bots" to automate repetitive and rule-based tasks within your organization. These bots mimic human actions, interacting with your existing software applications and systems to perform tasks with precision and speed. RPA is the key to achieving operational excellence, allowing your employees to focus on higher-value activities while leaving the mundane and time-consuming tasks to the bots.
